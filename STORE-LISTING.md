## Chrome Web Store

### Short Description (132 chars max)
Capture LinkedIn conversations and sync contacts to Affinity CRM with one click. Keyboard shortcut, notes, and dashboard included.

### Detailed Description
LinkedIn to Affinity bridges your LinkedIn conversations with Affinity CRM, helping dealflow teams stay organized without switching tabs.

**Key Features:**
- One-click sync: Send any LinkedIn conversation to Affinity as a formatted note
- Smart contact matching: Automatically finds existing contacts or creates new ones
- Keyboard shortcut: Press Ctrl+Shift+A to sync instantly
- Dashboard: View your lists, recent activity, and follow-up reminders
- Weekly summary export: Copy a markdown summary of your dealflow to clipboard
- Desktop notifications: Get notified when a sync completes

**How It Works:**
1. Open any conversation on LinkedIn Messaging
2. Click the "Send to Affinity" button (or use the keyboard shortcut)
3. The extension extracts the conversation, finds or creates the contact in Affinity, and adds the conversation as a note

**Privacy First:**
- Only activates on LinkedIn messaging pages
- Connects directly to the Affinity API using your own API key
- No data is sent to any third-party servers
- No tracking or analytics

**Requirements:**
- An Affinity CRM account with API access
- Your Affinity API key (found in Settings > API)

Built for dealflow teams, VCs, recruiters, and anyone who manages relationships in Affinity CRM.
